Professional Help

When should you seek it?

If persistent pain is making you feel overwhelmed, there are things you can talk to your doctor about. Talk to your doctor if:

  • You want to learn more about pain (that's not included on this website)
  • You're not coping with your pain
  • You're struggling at work with pain
  • You noticed sudden changes in your symptoms
    • Sudden increase in pain intensity
    • Sudden loss of muscle power in arms or legs
    • A lack of sensation anywhere in your body
    • Unexplained loss of weight
    • Sudden change in your ability to control your bladder/bowel
    • Sudden feeling of pins and needles or numbness in hands/feet

After speaking with your doctor, they may recommend you to seek help from specialized practitioners like physiatrists and physiotherapists.
